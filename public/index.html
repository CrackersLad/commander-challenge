    📜 How to Play & Features

    1. Setup & Rules: The Host sets the budget limits (Cmdr & Deck), EDHREC Rank limits, Max Bracket, and Format (Independent vs Pass & Pick). You can also add a Discord Webhook for automated alerts!

    2. Draft Your Commander: Roll random options, use your allowed Rerolls, or Manual Search for a legal commander. Turn on Blind Draft to keep picks secret until everyone locks in!

    3. Build & Seal: Brew on Archidekt or Moxfield and submit your link. We automatically verify Legality (100 cards), Price, Salt Score, and Bracket. Lock in your price once you're Battle Ready.

    4. Schedule the Match: Use the Availability Calendar to mark your free days. The Host can lock in a date, format, and prize, sending out Push/Discord notifications.

    5. Crown the Victor: After the game, the Host clicks Declare Winner. This records the victory on the champion's Player Profile, tracks lifetime wins, and resets the lobby for your next draft!
